# COPY — duplicate an object, transformed. before/after pair via hold.
grid 14 14
bg 0
spawn Lshape 3 at 2 2 color 3 id L
hold 2                         # BEFORE: just the original
copy L to 2 8 rot 1 tint 4    # AFTER: a rotated, recolored copy appears
copy L to 8 2 flip h tint 6   # and a mirrored copy
snap 2
