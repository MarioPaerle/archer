# TASK: remove the scattered noise, keep the solid shape.
# The noise is FREE content (scatter) — it is not a function of the shape; it just varies per example.
rule remove the scattered single-cell noise and keep the solid shape
concept denoising segmentation
vary flip rot zoom color
grid 14 12
bg 0
seed 1
spawn square rand 3 4 random 4 4 8 8 color rand id s
scatter 14 color rand box 0 0 11 13   # free noise sprinkled here and there
hold 1                 # IN: shape + noise
cut
remove shape noise     # OUT: clean shape
snap 1
