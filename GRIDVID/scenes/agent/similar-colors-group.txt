name similar colors group
# SIMILAR COLORS ENCODE SIMILAR INFO: color = group identity, and group identity
# = shared behaviour. The two GREEN squares behave alike (both fall together), the two
# YELLOW squares behave alike (both drift right together), and the lone RED one is its
# own group (stays put / falls alone). Same color -> same motion; different color -> different motion.
grid 18 22
gravity down 1
walls box
# GREEN group: both fall straight down (gravity on)
spawn square 2 at 1 2 color 3 grav 1
spawn square 2 at 1 14 color 3 grav 1
# YELLOW group: both translate rightward at the same constant velocity (no gravity)
spawn square 2 at 6 1 color 4 vel 0 1
spawn square 2 at 9 1 color 4 vel 0 1
# RED singleton: different color -> different (its own) behaviour: rises (vel up)
spawn square 2 at 18 8 color 2 vel -1 0
run 14
hold 5
