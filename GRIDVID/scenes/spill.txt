# SPILL — the new simple fluid: a laminar pour that fills a cup and overflows the rim.
grid 13 14
bg 0
walls floor
spawn line 5 v at 5 4 color 5      # left wall of the cup
spawn line 5 v at 5 8 color 5      # right wall
spawn line 5 at 9 4 color 5        # bottom
spill at 0 6 color 8 rate 1        # continuous pour from above
run 30
