# selection by extreme: recolor the smallest object.
rule recolor the smallest object to blue
concept selection size extreme
difficulty 0.4
vary flip rot color
grid 12 12
bg 0
seed 1
spawn square rand 2 4 random 0 0 5 5 color rand 3 9 id a
spawn square rand 2 4 random 6 0 11 5 color rand 3 9 id b
spawn square rand 2 4 random 0 6 5 11 color rand 3 9 id c
hold 1
cut
recolor smallest tint 1
snap 1
