# whole-grid: remap every cell by a fixed colour map (2<->6, 3<->4) — like real ARC 0d3d703e.
rule recolour the whole grid by the fixed map 2->6, 6->2, 3->4, 4->3
concept grid palette recolor whole-grid
difficulty 0.6
vary flip rot
grid 10 10
bg 0
seed 1
noise uniform colors 0 2 3 4 6
hold 1
cut
grid_map 2 6 6 2 3 4 4 3
