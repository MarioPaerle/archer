name sorter
# kids' shape-sorter: pieces spawn at random and travel to their matching hole,
# then snap/lock in place (goal-directed motion + shape matching). Seed varies layout.
grid 20 20
seed 3
sort on
hole square 3 at 2 2 id H1 color 5
hole plus 3 at 2 15 id H2 color 5
hole diamond 2 at 14 9 id H3 color 5
spawn square 3 random color 2 target H1
spawn plus 3 random color 6 target H2
spawn diamond 2 random color 7 target H3
run 34
hold 4
