# RULE (arithmetic predicate, grounded targets): each object is routed by the PARITY of its cell count.
# Even-area objects take the colour of the LEFT key swatch; odd-area objects take the RIGHT key swatch.
rule objects with an even number of cells become the colour of the left key; odd-celled objects the colour of the right key
concept parity counting dispatch symbol-grounding contextual
difficulty 0.72
grid rand 14 17 13 15
seed 1
spawn dot at 0 0 color 8 id keyeven
spawn dot at 0 2 color 6 id keyodd
spawn square rand 2 3 color rand 2 4 random 2 1 6 6
spawn plus rand 2 3 color rand 2 4 random 2 8 6 11
spawn rect rand 2 3 rand 2 3 color rand 2 4 random 8 1 11 6
spawn diamond 2 color rand 2 4 random 8 8 11 11
hold 1
cut
dispatch all by parity
  case even
    recolor it tint 8
  case odd
    recolor it tint 6
end
