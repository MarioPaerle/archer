# RULE (context-sensitive): recolor each shape by a PREDICATE of its own form —
# symmetric shapes turn blue (1), asymmetric shapes turn red (2). The incidental
# input colour (3..7) and the positions vary per example; only the symmetry decides the OUT.
rule symmetric shapes become blue, asymmetric shapes become red
concept dispatch predicate symmetry classification conditional
difficulty 0.6
grid rand 14 18 14 18
seed 1
spawn square rand 2 3 color rand 3 7 random 1 1 6 6
spawn plus 3 color rand 3 7 random 1 8 6 12
spawn Lshape 3 color rand 3 7 random 8 1 12 6
spawn triangle 4 color rand 3 7 random 8 8 12 12
hold 1
cut
dispatch all by symmetric
  case yes
    recolor it tint 1
  case no
    recolor it tint 2
end
snap 1
vary flip rot zoom
