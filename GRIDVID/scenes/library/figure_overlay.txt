# RULE: superimpose the two figures; cells only in A keep A's colour, cells only in B keep B's colour,
# and the SHARED cells take the special overlap colour (5). (overlay, not xor — overlap is marked, not cancelled.)
rule overlay the two shapes; shared cells become the overlap colour
concept boolean figure-algebra overlay intersection mark
difficulty 0.7
grid rand 12 15 10 12
seed 1
spawn square rand 3 5 at 1 1 color 2 id A
spawn plus rand 3 5 at 1 7 color 3 id B
hold 1
cut
overlay_figs A B at 1 1 overlap 5
snap 1
vary flip rot
