# RULE: count the objects that enclose a hole. The OUT is a compact yellow tally bar.
# The answer colour is grounded: the holed frame objects are already yellow in the IN.
rule count the objects that enclose a hole; output one yellow cell per holed object
concept counting predicate topology loop holes tally
difficulty 0.6
grid 22 15
seed 1
repeat rand 2 4 i
  spawn frame rand 3 4 rand 3 4 at 1 $i*4+1 color 4
end
spawn ring 3 at 7 2 color 4
repeat rand 2 4 j
  spawn square rand 2 3 at 10 $j*4+1 color 3
end
hold 1
cut
tally where loop at 5 2 color 4 dir h
remove shape frame
remove shape ring
remove shape square
snap 1
vary flip
