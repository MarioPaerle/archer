# RULE: convex shapes are kept and turn green (3); concave shapes (L / frame / notch)
# turn red (2). The detector is `convex` (hull == filled).
rule convex shapes turn green, concave shapes turn red
concept dispatch predicate convexity classification conditional
difficulty 0.6
grid rand 15 18 15 18
seed 1
spawn disc 2 color rand 4 8 random 1 1 6 6
spawn plus 3 color rand 4 8 random 1 9 6 13
spawn Lshape 3 color rand 4 8 random 9 1 13 6
spawn notch 3 color rand 4 8 random 9 9 13 13
hold 1
cut
dispatch all by convex
  case yes
    recolor it tint 3
  case no
    recolor it tint 2
end
snap 1
vary flip rot zoom
