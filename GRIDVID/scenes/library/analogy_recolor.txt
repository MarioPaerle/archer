# RULE (analogy A:B::C:?): the top pair shows a recolour (A -> B). Apply the SAME change to C.
# bind_transform captures the A->B delta; apply re-applies it to C. Colours vary per example.
rule the top shows how one shape changes colour; apply the same change to the bottom shape
concept analogy bind apply transform recolor
difficulty 0.7
grid 12 10
seed 1
spawn square 2 at 1 1 color rand 2 5 id A
spawn square 2 at 1 7 color rand 6 9 id B
spawn square 2 at 6 1 color rand 2 5 id C
hold 1
cut
bind_transform T from A to B
apply T to C
snap 1
vary flip
